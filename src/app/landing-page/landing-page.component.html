About Us

Poker Sports League is India’s first poker league. The idea behind PSL is to sportify the game of poker through a unique, team based format. This is the only sports format where each team has a combination of professionals and amateurs. Absolutely anybody who knows the basics of poker - can play a series of on-ground or online poker games to secure a spot in this India wide poker league. All for FREE. We have big prize pools, no entry fee and a hell of a competition ahead.
Poker Sports League is the world’s largest poker league in terms of Prizepool and Participation.

HOW TO APPLY

Online Qualifiers start on November 14th, 2019 on adda52.com . There will be a total of 72 online games out of which a player is permitted to play a maximum of 18 . 100 % of players will be awarded leaderboard points .

Post the 72 qualifiers the top 90 players make it to the leaderboard
